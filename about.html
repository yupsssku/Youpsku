Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FreeNumber

🚀 Getting Started

❓ How do I use FreeNumber?
It's simple! Visit our homepage, browse available phone numbers from different countries, copy the number you want, and use it anywhere for verification. SMS messages will appear automatically on our website in real-time. No registration required!
💰 Is FreeNumber really free?
Yes! FreeNumber is 100% free. No registration, no subscription, no hidden charges. We're supported by non-intrusive advertising. You can access all features without paying a single cent.
📝 Do I need to create an account?
Nope! FreeNumber requires zero registration. Just visit our website, pick a number, and start receiving SMS immediately. We believe in keeping things simple and accessible.

📱 Usage Questions

⏱️ Why isn't my SMS showing up?
SMS usually appear within seconds, but sometimes can take up to 2-3 minutes. If you still don't see it after waiting, try another number or refresh the page. Some services may not work with our numbers due to their anti-spam filters.
🔄 Can I receive SMS to the same number multiple times?
Yes, you can! However, keep in mind that numbers are shared with all users. Multiple people might be using the same number simultaneously. We recommend using numbers for one-time verification only.
✅ Which services work with FreeNumber?
Our numbers work with most popular services including WhatsApp, Telegram, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, Discord, and hundreds of others. However, some services (especially banking and financial apps) may block virtual numbers for security reasons.
🌍 How many countries are supported?
We currently support 25+ countries including USA, UK, Canada, Indonesia, India, Nigeria, Philippines, Pakistan, and many more. We're constantly adding new countries based on user demand.
⏰ How long are messages kept?
SMS messages are automatically deleted after 24-48 hours for privacy and storage reasons. Make sure to save any important verification codes before they disappear!

⚙️ Technical Questions

🚫 Why was a number blocked or removed?
Numbers may be temporarily blocked if they receive too many messages, are used for spam, or are flagged by service providers. They're usually rotated back into service after a cooling period. We also regularly update our number pool for better service quality.
📲 Does FreeNumber work on mobile devices?
Absolutely! FreeNumber is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Use it anywhere, anytime!
🔌 Do you have an API?
Currently, we don't offer a public API. If you're interested in API access for legitimate development or business use cases, please contact us with details about your project.
🐛 I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at support@freenumber.site with details about the bug, including your browser, device, and steps to reproduce it. We appreciate your help in improving our service!

🔒 Privacy & Security

👀 Are the messages private?
No! All messages are PUBLIC and visible to everyone on our website. Never use our service for sensitive or private information like banking details, passwords, medical records, or government documents.
⚠️ What should I NOT use FreeNumber for?
Never use FreeNumber to receive: banking or credit card information, important account passwords, medical or health records, government documents or IDs, social security numbers, tax information, or any confidential/sensitive data. Use it only for non-critical, temporary verification.
🛡️ Is FreeNumber safe to use?
FreeNumber is safe for non-sensitive verification purposes. However, remember that all messages are public. Use it for testing apps, temporary signups, or non-critical verifications only. Never share personal or sensitive information through our service.
📊 Do you track my usage?
We use minimal analytics (Google Analytics) to understand traffic and improve our service. We don't collect personal information or track individual users. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.
